# Interview Prep Examples

This folder contains **example** interview preparation materials for different companies and candidates. These are templates to show the structure and depth expected for interviews.

## What's in here:

- `google_example.md` — Interview prep for a **Systems/Infrastructure Engineer** interview at Google
- `amazon_example.md` — Interview prep for a **Backend/E-commerce Engineer** interview at Amazon  
- `stripe_example.md` — Interview prep for a **Payments Engineer** interview at Stripe

## How to use these examples:

1. **Pick a company you're interviewing with** (Google, Amazon, Stripe, Netflix, etc.)
2. **Copy the structure** of one of these examples
3. **Customize it** with YOUR tech stack, projects, and company-specific knowledge
4. **Highlight 2-3 projects** that are most relevant to the company's domain
5. **Research company-specific questions** and prepare answers

## Key sections in each example:

- **Self Introduction** — 2 minutes, 3 parts: 1) Who you are + GPA, 2) Tech stack, 3) Relevant projects + why you're excited
- **Project Walkthrough** — Deep dive into your best project (architecture, why it matters for this company)
- **Company-Specific Q&A** — Questions you're likely to face tailored to that company's business

## Structure Tips:

✅ **Start with technical depth** (Python, Go, AWS, etc.)  
✅ **Then project impact** (scaled to X users, reduced latency by Y%)  
✅ **Then company fit** (why YOUR stack matches THEIR problems)  
✅ **Answer company-specific questions** (not generic question bank)
